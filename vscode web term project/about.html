Shoes4Dudes

What we do here at Shoes4Dudes is we take old worn down shoes, and fix them up to the best of our ability to then sell at a lower more affordable price for all to enjoy.
We just recently opened up our site to overseas foreign countries with international shipping.

Our Story

The very first idea/rough drafts for our company originally started back in 2016 and actually started in our little garage in sarnia ontario.
Our uncle made shoes from scratch and taught us at a young age how to make and repair many problems that can happen to your shoes.
Ever since then we started a repair service and eventually thought one day what if we expanded repairng shoes to be almost like brand new and sell to those who need.
Ever since then we have grown with your support you've shown us and we appreciate it more than anything.
